Data Analysis

I specialize in transforming complex company information into accessible and easily updatable formats, ensuring all stakeholders have access to the data they need to make informed decisions. To achieve this, I create interactive dashboards using both R and Tableau.

Tableau Dashboards

One example is a Tableau dashboard designed for a company to monitor their Amazon competitors' data, including market share and its evolution over time. Users can filter the dashboard by brand and month-year, allowing for customized analysis and insights.



R Dashboards

Another dashboard is built using R and deployed as a Shiny app. It provides the company with comprehensive inventory projections for the fiscal year, along with the corresponding financial budget required to meet those inventory needs. The app offers powerful filtering capabilities, enabling users to explore data based on sales channels, month-year, and SKU. Additionally, the data can be easily downloaded to support further analysis and reporting.
