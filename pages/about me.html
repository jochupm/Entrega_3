About me


Hello there! I'm Emma Johnson, a passionate professional with a diverse background and a keen interest in the world of data and technology. I hold a Bachelor's degree in International Trade, a technical degree in Systems Analysis, and a specialization certificate in Data Analytics, proudly issued by Google. With over a decade of experience in supply chain management, I've gained valuable insights into the intricate workings of global markets. Throughout my journey, I've also embraced the dynamic realm of data analytics, serving as a proficient data analyst for several years. Witnessing firsthand how data can drive informed decisions and unlock untapped potential, I've developed a deep appreciation for its significance in today's business landscape. My expertise doesn't stop there. I'm currently focusing on web development and expanding my services to assist small and medium companies in establishing their online presence. By harnessing my technical skills and creative flair, I aim to empower businesses with customized websites that showcase their unique offerings and enhance their digital footprint. I firmly believe that the seamless integration of data analysis and web development can truly transform the way companies operate, allowing them to thrive in the digital age. Through my services, I aspire to be a driving force in helping businesses unlock new opportunities and propel towards success. Let's embark on a journey together, where data-driven insights meet innovative web solutions, and where your business can truly shine in the digital world.
